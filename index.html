The first generation of Pokémon, often referred to as Generation I, was released in Japan in 1996 and internationally in 1998. It introduced a total of 151 Pokémon species, including iconic characters such as Pikachu, Charmander, and Squirtle. The gameplay involved training and battling Pokémon, collecting gym badges, and ultimately becoming the Champion of the Pokémon League. The first generation games, Pokémon Red, Blue, and Green, were released for the Game Boy and have since become a beloved cultural phenomenon with a lasting impact on gaming and pop culture.

Welcome to the Random Team Generator! Here you can press a button to generate a team of 6 random Pokémon to use in your adventures.

To generate your team, simply press the "Generate Team" button. The system will randomly select 6 Pokémon from the first two generations and display them on the screen.

If you don't like the Pokémon that were generated, don't worry! You have two chances to change your team. Simply click the "Change Team" button and the system will generate a new set of 6 Pokémon. You can do this a maximum of two times.
